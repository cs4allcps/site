CS4All Staff
Brenda Darden Wilkerson is a technology education evangelist and the Director of Computer Science & IT Education at Chicago Public Schools. As the founder of the original Computer Science for All initiative in 2013, her work includes building CS into CPS’ general course of study. Brenda is a graduate of Northwestern University where she studied computer science.
